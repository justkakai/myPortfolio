About

Hi there!

I'm Kakai, a full-stack web developer born and bred in Nairobi, now living in Berlin. It's nice to see you in this tiny corner of the internet.

My love for the kitschiness and playfulness that is 90's and Y2K aesthetics is what first sparked my interest in web development. My desire to know how and why initially landed me at Codecademy, then later on at the Digital Career Institute (DCI) in Berlin.
